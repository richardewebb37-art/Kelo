## ğŸ¨ Design System Overview

### Colors
- **Blueprint Blue**: #0066CC (primary accent)
- **Blueprint Dark**: #001F3F (background)
- **White Line**: #FFFFFF (text and lines)
- **Yellow Highlight**: #FFD700 (construction accent)
- **Grid Line**: rgba(255,255,255,0.2) (subtle grid)

### Typography
- **Headings**: Orbitron (bold, technical)
- **Body**: Roboto Mono (monospace, readable)
- **Accent**: Architects Daughter (hand-drawn annotations)

### Key Features
- Blueprint grid background (20px fine, 100px major)
- Corner brackets on cards
- Technical stamps (APPROVED, DRAFT, etc.)
- Measurement lines with labels
- Glow effects (blue and yellow)
- Construction-themed badges
